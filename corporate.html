Core Focus: Life-Saving Airbag Deployment

Bounce Safety Systems LLC is dedicated to a singular, critical mission: engineering high-reliability electronic deployment brains for personal airbag systems. Because a human life can depend on milliseconds, our hardware and software architecture is explicitly designed from the ground up to target zero-fail, mission-critical execution.

Core Competencies

We design and engineer ultra-high-reliability, real-time embedded safety electronics for critical mechanical deployments. Specializing in advanced STM32 microcontroller architectures, we develop multi-layered sensor arrays featuring hardware and firmware redundancy to eliminate single points of failure in high-stakes environments.

  • Advanced Multi-Layer PCB Design: Experienced in complex, noise-immune 4-layer PCB routing layout optimizing signal integrity for low-voltage sensor lines right alongside high-current power networks.
  • Real-Time Embedded Firmware: Development of tight, microsecond-level firmware execution loops optimizing hardware interrupts, watchdogs, and status registers to ensure the system cannot lock up or fail mid-cycle.
  • Low-Latency Squib Firing Circuitry: Designing safe, robust Infineon MOSFET-based actuator firing paths engineered to deliver high-current energy bursts instantly when deployment criteria are met.

Differentiators & Core Architecture

  • Triple Modular Redundancy (TMR): Engineered a proprietary hardware/firmware architecture utilizing three independent IMUs tracking simultaneously with a real-time two-out-of-three voting system to eliminate single points of failure, preventing accidental triggers or missed deployments.
  • High-Speed Sensor Fusion: Microsecond-level telemetry processing loops capable of executing instantaneous, high-frequency decision logic for real-time deployment.
  • Military-Grade Systems DNA: Built on a career foundation managing high-consequence technology environments—including military-base weapon systems guidance, cruise missiles, and GPS technologies—bringing a rigorous, disciplined approach to personal protection safety systems.

Research & Development Pipeline

Bounce Safety Systems LLC is actively expanding its R&D portfolio with high-utility, secure technologies:

  • FLOAT: A wearable, water-activated life raft deployment system designed for maritime safety.
  • SPOOKY: A secure, pulse-based communication technology utilizing sub-Nyquist sampling and sparse signal reconstruction. This system ensures communications appear as scrambled noise to unauthorized interceptors, providing a high-security layer for sensitive data transmissions.
